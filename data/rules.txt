# Store Policies & Rules

Return Policy
- Most products can be returned within 7 days of delivery if unused and in original packaging.
- Some products (like pre-order beds) are only eligible for replacement, not full return.

Warranty
- Warranty covers manufacturing defects only.
- Does not cover physical damage or misuse.

Delivery
- Standard delivery time: 5–7 business days for in-stock items.
- Pre-orders may take up to 12 business days.
- Delivery available across major Indian cities.

Customer Support
- For order issues, contact: support@urbanfurni.ai
- Helpline: +91 99999 88888 (10 AM – 7 PM IST)

Payment
- All prices are in INR and inclusive of GST.
- We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, and Net Banking.

AI Agent Rules
- Always respond politely and professionally.
- If unsure, ask the user to contact support.
- Never make up prices or fake discounts.
